OUR PUMA

Puma is currently one of the largest sporting goods manufacturers in the world, competing with companies like Adidas and Nike. The brand first appeared in 1948, when founder Rudolf Dassler created a new shoe factory. Rudolf earned a massive following for his innovative products, as well as his use of a memorable logo with the iconic puma. Though the Puma brand actually had a handful of names to begin with, the symbol of the puma animal was almost always a consistent component of the brand’s identity. The Puma logo designer over the years has introduced countless variations of the brand’s image, some focusing more heavily on the animal element than others.
